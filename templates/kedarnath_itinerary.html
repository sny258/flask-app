Day 1: Delhi to Haridwar (230 km)

  • Departure from Delhi in the morning.
  • Arrival in Haridwar by evening.
  • Attend the Ganga Aarti at Har Ki Pauri.
  • Overnight stay at a hotel in Haridwar.

Day 2: Haridwar to Guptkashi (205 km)

  • Early morning departure from Haridwar.
  • Drive through scenic routes via Rishikesh, Devprayag, and Rudraprayag.
  • Arrival in Guptkashi by evening.
  • Overnight stay at a hotel in Guptkashi.

Day 3: Guptkashi to Kedarnath (30 km drive + 16 km trek)

  • Early morning drive 30 km to Gaurikund.
  • Trek from Gaurikund to Kedarnath (approximately 16 km).
  • Arrival in Kedarnath by afternoon.
  • Visit Kedarnath Temple and attend evening Aarti.
  • Overnight stay in Kedarnath.

Day 4: Kedarnath to Guptkashi (16 km trek + 30 km drive)

  • Attend the morning Aarti at Kedarnath Temple.
  • Trek back to Gaurikund (16 km).
  • Drive back to Guptkashi (30 km).
  • Overnight stay at a hotel in Guptkashi.

Day 5: Guptkashi to Rishikesh (170 km)

  • Morning departure from Guptkashi.
  • Drive to Rishikesh via Rudraprayag and Devprayag.
  • Arrival in Rishikesh by afternoon.
  • Explore Rishikesh and visit the famous Laxman Jhula and Ram Jhula.
  • Overnight stay at a hotel in Rishikesh.

Day 6: Rishikesh to Delhi (240 km)

  • Morning departure from Rishikesh.
  • Drive back to Delhi.
  • Arrival in Delhi by evening.
