Day 1: Delhi to Leh (By Flight)

  • Take an early morning flight from Delhi to Leh.
  • Arrival in Leh by late morning.
  • Acclimatize to the high altitude.
  • Explore Leh Market and surroundings.
  • Overnight stay at a hotel in Leh.

Day 2: Leh Local Sightseeing

  • Visit Leh Palace and Shanti Stupa.
  • Explore Hall of Fame Museum.
  • Enjoy the local cuisine.
  • Overnight stay in Leh.

Day 3: Leh to Nubra Valley via Khardung La Pass (160 km)

  • Drive to Nubra Valley, passing through Khardung La Pass.
  • Visit Diskit Monastery and enjoy camel safari in Hunder.
  • Explore the beauty of Nubra Valley.
  • Overnight stay in a camp or guesthouse in Nubra Valley.

Day 4: Nubra Valley to Leh (160 km)

  • Drive back to Leh via Khardung La Pass.
  • Explore the local markets in Leh.
  • Rest and relax in the evening.
  • Overnight stay in Leh.

Day 5: Leh to Pangong Lake (224 km)

  • Departure from Leh after breakfast.
  • Drive to Pangong Lake via Chang La Pass.
  • Enjoy the mesmerizing beauty of Pangong Lake.
  • Overnight stay in camps near Pangong Lake.

Day 6: Pangong Lake to Leh (224 km)

  • Drive back to Leh after breakfast.
  • Visit Hemis Monastery and Thiksey Monastery on the way.
  • Arrival in Leh by afternoon.
  • Free time for shopping or relaxation.
  • Overnight stay in Leh.

Day 7: Leh to Sarchu (253 km)

  • Early morning departure from Leh.
  • Drive to Sarchu via Tanglang La Pass.
  • Enjoy the scenic beauty of the landscape.
  • Arrival in Sarchu by evening.
  • Overnight stay in camps at Sarchu.

Day 8: Sarchu to Manali (222 km)

  • Departure from Sarchu after breakfast.
  • Drive to Manali, crossing Baralacha La Pass and Rohtang Pass.
  • Enjoy the scenic drive and stop at picturesque locations for photography.
  • Arrival in Manali by evening.
  • Overnight stay at a hotel in Manali.

Day 9: Manali to Delhi (536 km)

  • Departure from Manali in the morning.
  • Drive back to Delhi.
  • Arrival in Delhi by evening.
  • End of the memorable Leh Ladakh tour.
