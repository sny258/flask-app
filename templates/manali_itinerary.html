Day 1: Delhi to Manali (536 km)

  • Departure from Delhi early in the morning.
  • Drive to Manali, enjoying the scenic views along the way.
  • Arrival in Manali by evening.
  • Check-in at the hotel and relax.
  • Explore Mall Road in the evening.
  • Overnight stay at a hotel in Manali.

Day 2: Manali Local Sightseeing

  • Visit Hadimba Devi Temple, Manu Temple, and Vashisht Hot Water Springs.
  • Explore Tibetan Monastery and Club House.
  • Enjoy adventure activities like paragliding and river rafting (optional).
  • Overnight stay in Manali.

Day 3: Manali to Kasol (75 km)

  • Departure from Manali after breakfast.
  • Drive to Kasol, a picturesque village on the banks of the Parvati River.
  • Explore the cafes, markets, and scenic beauty of Kasol.
  • Overnight stay at a hotel or guesthouse in Kasol.

Day 4: Kasol to Tosh Trek and Return (20 km)

  • Embark on a trek to Tosh, a scenic village known for its hippie culture.
  • Enjoy the picturesque views of the Parvati Valley during the trek.
  • Explore Tosh village and relax by the riverside.
  • Return to Kasol by evening.
  • Overnight stay in Kasol.

Day 5: Kasol to Sissu (145 km)

  • Departure from Kasol after breakfast.
  • Drive to Sissu, a scenic village in Lahaul Valley.
  • Enjoy the serene beauty of the Himalayas and Chandra River.
  • Explore the local culture and cuisine.
  • Overnight stay at a hotel or guesthouse in Sissu.

Day 6: Sissu to Jibhi (90 km)

  • Departure from Sissu after breakfast.
  • Drive to Jibhi, a quaint village known for its natural beauty.
  • Visit Jibhi Waterfall and explore the nearby forest trails.
  • Relax by the river and enjoy the peaceful atmosphere.
  • Overnight stay at a guesthouse or homestay in Jibhi.

Day 7: Jibhi to Delhi (525 km)

  • Departure from Jibhi in the morning.
  • Drive back to Delhi.
  • Arrival in Delhi by evening.
  • End of the memorable journey.
